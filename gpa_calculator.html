GPA Calculator

Grading Scale: A+ = 4.33, A = 4.00, A- = 3.67, B+ = 3.33, B = 3.00, B- = 2.67, C+ = 2.33, C = 2.00, C- = 1.67, D = 1.00, F = 0.00

Single Year vs. Multi-Year:
Single Year calculates GPA for one academic year.
Multi-Year calculates cumulative GPA over multiple years.

Weighted vs. Unweighted GPA:
Unweighted GPA is calculated on a standard 4.0 scale.
Weighted GPA adds extra points for Honors/AP courses.
